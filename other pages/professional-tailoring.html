Professional Tailoring

This advanced course is designed for those who want to master the art of professional tailoring. You will learn advanced techniques in custom fittings, haute couture, and garment construction. By the end of the course, you will be able to create high-quality, custom-tailored garments for clients.

Our experienced instructors will guide you through hands-on projects, ensuring you gain practical skills that you can apply immediately. Whether you're tailoring for personal use or considering a career in fashion, this course is the perfect next step.

The course also covers the basics of fabric selection, pattern making, and sewing machine maintenance. You'll leave with the confidence to tackle your own tailoring projects at home.

Course Requirements

Intermediate sewing experience is required. All materials and tools will be provided, but you are welcome to bring your own sewing machine if you prefer.

  • Intermediate understanding of sewing tools and materials.
  • Willingness to learn and practice regularly.
  • Intermediate sewing experience needed.

What You'll Learn

By the end of this course, you will:

  • Understand how to create custom fittings.
  • Master advanced tailoring techniques.
  • Learn to construct haute couture garments.
  • Create your own tailored garments.
